My Engineering Portfolio

Piece Job

PieceJob is a backend service for a gig-work platform that connects job seekers with short-term, flexible work opportunities. It enables individuals to create profiles showcasing their skills and experience, while businesses can quickly post and manage temporary job listings. The system handles the entire workflow—from job posting and candidate matching to application management, job completion, and review—using a Spring Boot and PostgreSQL stack. It includes secure OAuth2 authentication, a clean REST API, and readiness for mobile integration, targeting accessibility for low-data users and emerging markets.
